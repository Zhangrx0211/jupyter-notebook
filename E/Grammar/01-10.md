# 05,06，07
    present simple:(有do/does)
    有个单三需要注意
    常见词：always,never often,usually,sometimes
    例句：I/we/you/they (like/do/have)..., he/she/it (likes/does/has)...
    否定句：I (don't) like/do/have...,     he (doesn't) like/do/have...
    一般疑问句：(Do) I/we/you/they like..., (Does) he/she/it like...
    特殊疑问句：特殊疑问词+do/does
# 08 区别
    I am doing(present continuous) = now 
    否定句：I am not doing ..., 一般疑问句：Is you doing(be提前)
    I do (present simple) =general/all the time/sometims    
    否定句：I don't do ...     一般疑问句: Do you do ..(前面加do/does)
