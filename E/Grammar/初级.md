# 29-36
    models情态动词：may/might， can/could， must/mustn't, should
    主 + 情态动词 (not)+ v原型 
    一般疑问句：情态动词 + 主 + v原型
    might(可能性不是很高)
    I may = I might
    May I ask a question?(我能不能问一个问题，很有礼貌)

    I can do sth,(我可以做这件事)  persent simple(现在时)
    When I was yong,I could run very fast。 past(过去时)
    疑问句中不要吧could看作过去时的形式，比can稍微温婉一些
    Can you...? Could you...? (请求对方做sth)
    Can I have...? Can I get? (向对方要求sth)
    Can I..？ Could I...？ (向对方征求意见)

    I must do sth = I need to do it (我需要去做sth)
    I'm very hungry. I must eat something.
    在过去的时间点：has to... 不用must
    I was very hungry. I had to eat something,
    I mustn't do sth(一定不要做某事)
    I don't need to do sth (没有必要做这个事情)
    You don't need to go,(你没有必要走)
    You mustn't go.(你不能走)

    You should do something = it is a good/right thing to do
    例：It's a good film, You should go and see it.
    You should n't do something = not a good thing to do
    例：Tom shouldn't go to bed so late
    We often say：I think...should...
    例：It's late. I think I should go home now
    I don't think... should ...
    例：I don't think you should work so hard.
    Do you think ... should...
    例：Do you think I should buy this hat?
    should = out to

    I have to do sth(我得去...) = it is necessary for me to do it,
    past: had to...
    negatives: I/He don't/doesn't have to do ...
    question: Do/Did I have to do ....(句首直接加Do/Did)
    have to \ must:个人主观看法
    have to: 可以只陈述客观事实
