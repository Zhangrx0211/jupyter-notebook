# 05,06，07 (present simple)
    present simple:(有do/does)

    例句：I/we/you/they (like/do/have)..., he/she/it (likes/does/has)...

    一般疑问句：(Do) I/we/you/they like..., (Does) he/she/it like...
    特殊疑问句：特殊疑问词+do/does

# 15 16 17 18 (present perfect)
    present perfect 强调过去对现在的影响
    例句：I have done... = I have ved，I've cleaned my shoes. 
    否定句：I haven't done..., He/She/It hasn't done...
    一般疑问句：Have I done ...,Has He/She/It done...

    经常出现：
    I've just... (在很短的一段时间之前，我刚刚...) They've just arrived.
    I've already...(我已经...) They've already arried
    I haven't ... yet.(直到现在还没有...) They haven't arried yet
    一般疑问句：Have they arried yet? 他们已经到了吗？

    Have you been to France?(你有没有去过法国)
    Have you ever been to France?(你有没有[在任何时候]去过日本)
    ever(疑问句使用：在任何时候=曾经)。never(从来没有)
    I've been to(我去过)，He has gone to(他去了)

    How long have you lived in London?(你住在伦敦多久了？)
    She has lived there for three days(她已经住了3天了)
    How long have you been learning  German?(你学德语多久了，从过去持续到现在，未来还会继续)
    I‘ve been learning German for two years. (我学德语2年了)现在完成进行式
    How long has he been watching  TV?
    He has been watching TV since 5 o'clock

# 29-36
    models情态动词：may/might， can/could， must/mustn't, 
    主 + 情态动词 (not)+ v原型 
    一般疑问句：情态动词 + 主 + v原型
    might(可能性不是很高)
    I may = I might
    May I ask a question?(我能不能问一个问题，很有礼貌)

    I can do sth,(我可以做这件事)  persent simple(现在时)
    When I was yong,I could run very fast。 past(过去时)
    疑问句中不要吧could看作过去时的形式，比can稍微温婉一些
    Can you...? Could you...? (请求对方做sth)
    Can I have...? Can I get? (向对方要求sth)
    Can I..？ Could I...？ (向对方征求意见)

    I must do sth = I need to do it (我需要去做sth)
    I'm very hungry. I must eat something.
    在过去的时间点：has to... 不用must
    I was very hungry. I had to eat something,
    I mustn't do sth(一定不要做某事)
    I don't need to do sth (没有必要做这个事情)
    You don't need to go,(你没有必要走)
    You mustn't go.(你不能走)
