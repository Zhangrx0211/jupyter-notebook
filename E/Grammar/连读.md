# 09
    特殊连读，变音

    t+j = tf
    next year   meet you   got you ~ go chou
    I want you car ~ I wan chyou car
    Won't you come with me? ~ Won chyou com with me?
    Don't you know that? ~ Don chyou know that? 

    d+j = d3
    graduate   did you   would you

    s+j = 3f
    pleasure   miss you   bless you
